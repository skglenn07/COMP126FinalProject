Buying Guide

Snake Plant

The snake plant is a popular houseplant with slender, pointed leaves that come in a variety of green shades. The snake plant is a great beginner’s plant: it’s easy to grow, tolerant of drought and inconsistent watering, and flourishes even in areas with lower light. This is an ideal low-maintenance plant to help you develop your green thumb!

Easy

Aloe Vera

Aloe Vera is a succulent plant that is easy to care for, making it an excellent choice for beginners. It requires minimal maintenance as it thrives in indirect sunlight and only needs to be watered when the soil is completely dry. Aloe Vera also has a number of health benefits, such as soothing burns and promoting skin healing!

Easy

Fiddle-Leaf Fig

Fiddle-leaf figs are an excellent option for plant parents with some experience. These beautiful plants have large, glossy leaves that resemble the shape of a fiddle. Fiddle-leaf figs thrive in bright, indirect light and require regular watering. They prefer a well-draining potting mix and benefit from occasional fertilization. With proper care and attention, fiddle-leaf figs can grow into stunning statement pieces!

Intermediate

Monstera

Monstera plants are a great choice for plant parents who have some experience. They are known for their large, glossy leaves and unique fenestrations. With their moderate care requirements, they strike a balance between being low-maintenance and providing a rewarding challenge for intermediate plant owners. Monstera plants thrive in bright, indirect light and prefer well-draining soil. They appreciate regular watering, allowing the top inch of soil to dry out between waterings.

Intermediate

Elephant Ear Plant

Elephant ear plants, also known as Alocasia or Colocasia, provide experienced plant owners with an opportunity to expand their knowledge and skills in plant care. These stunning plants feature large, heart-shaped leaves that resemble the ears of an elephant. Elephant ear plants offer a challenge and require specific care to thrive. These plants prefer bright, indirect light and a warm, humid environment.

Advanced

Orchid

Orchids are a fantastic choice for seasoned plant parents. These beautiful flowers come in a wide variety of colors, shapes, and sizes, making them a visually captivating addition to any plant collection. Orchids require a delicate balance of light, temperature, humidity, and watering, making them a rewarding challenge for those who have honed their green thumb.

Advanced
